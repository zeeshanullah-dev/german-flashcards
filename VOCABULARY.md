## Study Tips for B1 TELC Exam

1. **Start with high-frequency categories:**
   - Common verbs (50+ essential verbs included)
   - Daily routines and time expressions
   - Common adjectives

2. **Focus on articles for nouns:**
   - All nouns include their correct article (der/die/das)
   - Critical for B1 level accuracy

3. **Use example sentences:**
   - Every card has context
   - Learn usage patterns, not just translations

4. **Study regularly:**
   - 10-15 minutes daily is better than cramming
   - The spaced repetition algorithm optimizes retention

5. **Practice all skills:**
   - Use the 🔊 Listen feature for pronunciation
   - Read example sentences aloud
   - Try to create your own sentences
