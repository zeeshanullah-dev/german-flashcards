# Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started

Your German Flashcards app is ready with **300+ B1 vocabulary words** already loaded!

### Start the App

```bash
cd ~/Documents/learning/german-flashcards
npm run dev
```

Then open: **http://localhost:5173**

## 📖 First Time Setup

The app automatically loads with 300+ vocabulary words on first use. You don't need to add anything!

### Your First Study Session

1. **Click on a flashcard** to see the German word
2. **Think of the answer** (the English translation)
3. **Click the card again** to flip and reveal the answer
4. **Rate your knowledge:**
   - ❌ **Again** - Forgot it completely
   - 😓 **Hard** - Difficult to remember
   - 👍 **Good** - Got it right
   - ✅ **Easy** - Very easy

5. The app automatically shows you the next card to study

## 🔊 Audio Feature

Click the **🔊 Listen** button on any card to hear the German pronunciation!

## 📊 Track Your Progress

Click the **Stats** tab to see:
- Total vocabulary cards
- How many you've studied
- How many you've mastered
- Your study streak 🔥
- Progress by category

## 🎯 Study Tips

### For Best Results:
- ✅ Study **10-15 minutes daily** (better than long sessions)
- ✅ Focus on **one rating button click** per card
- ✅ Be **honest** with your ratings (helps the algorithm)
- ✅ Use the **Listen button** to improve pronunciation
- ✅ Read the **example sentences** to learn context

### If You Make a Mistake:
- ✅ Use **🔄 Reset** in the Manage tab to start over
- ✅ Cards you rate as "Again" will show up sooner
- ✅ Cards you rate as "Easy" will show up later

## 🔄 Reset Your Progress

If you want to start fresh:

1. Go to **Manage** tab
2. Click **🔄 Reset** button
3. Confirm the reset
4. All 300+ cards will be restored and progress cleared

## 💾 Backup Your Data

Your progress is stored in your browser. To prevent data loss:

1. Go to **Manage** tab
2. Click **📥 Export**
3. Save the JSON file somewhere safe
4. To restore: Click **📤 Import** and select your saved file

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- **Don't clear browser data** - you'll lose your progress!
- **Export regularly** to backup your progress
- The app works **offline** after first load
- Your data **never leaves your computer** (100% private)
